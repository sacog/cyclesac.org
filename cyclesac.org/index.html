CycleSac is a project SACOG.

The CycleSac app is based on the CycleTracks app originally developed for the San Francisco Country Transportation Authority.

CycleSac is a smartphone app for recording your bicycle trips. When you use the app, you are giving transportation planners in Sacramento the data they need to make the region a better place to ride.

The app uses your phone's GPS record your routes in real-time, allowing the City of Sacramento to know which routes cyclists prefer. The app will also allow users to report problems along their route such as potholes, obstructed bike lanes, etc. The information collected by the app will be used by the City of Atlanta to make strategic improvements to bicycle infrastructure. This will make cycling in Atlanta more pleasant, and encourage those who currently don’t bike to give it a go.
